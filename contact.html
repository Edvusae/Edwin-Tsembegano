Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you provide a quote?

We provide free quotes within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent requests, call us directly at (555) 555-CLEAN for immediate assistance.

Do you bring your own cleaning supplies and equipment?

Yes! We bring all professional-grade equipment and eco-friendly cleaning products. If you prefer us to use specific products you provide, just let us know.

What if I'm not satisfied with the cleaning?

We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with any aspect of our service, we'll return within 24 hours to re-clean at no additional charge.

Are your cleaners insured and background-checked?

Absolutely. All our team members undergo thorough background checks, drug testing, and are covered by our $2 million liability insurance and bonding.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning?

Not at all! Many of our clients provide us with access instructions and are away during cleaning. We can work around your schedule for maximum convenience.
